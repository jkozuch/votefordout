What is this?

We are a citizen-led coalition of Ontarians who have witnessed the negative effects of Doug Ford's lack of leadership and harmful social policies.

These policies have taken the lives of many Ontarians and endangered the lives of our most at-risk populations.

We deserve better from our elected officials.
